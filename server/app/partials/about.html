Instituto Nacional de Bioinformatica

APPRIS has been developed within the GENCODE consortium to annotate alternative gene products with reliable biologically relevant data. GENCODE provides high-accuracy manual annotations of protein-coding loci and alternative variants as part of the ENCODE project. The GENCODE annotations are gradually replacing and augmenting the Ensembl automatic annotations.
